Loud (character)

Loud is the flagship character of the Loud Comics universe, brought to life through the imagination of comic book creator Patric G. Known for his resilience, street smarts, and supernatural roar, Loud navigates personal challenges while protecting the city from dark forces.

Background

Loud was once an ordinary teenager until a mysterious event imbued him with the power to unleash sonic force with his voice. This gift — or curse — has made him a vigilante icon and a target of secret organizations.

Loud wrestles with the morality of his powers, the loss of his family, and the constant push-pull between his human side and a supernatural destiny he barely understands.

Deep lore connects Loud to ancient myths, secret societies, and a prophecy known only as “The Echoverse.” Through flashbacks and coded scrolls, the comic reveals the layers behind his origin and enemies.

Publication History

Patric G began sketching Loud during his teen years. The comic debuted independently in the early 2020s, gathering a loyal fanbase online before expanding to indie publishing circuits. Loud Comics #1 introduced the character in full form, with stylized art and gritty storytelling that captured readers’ attention immediately.
